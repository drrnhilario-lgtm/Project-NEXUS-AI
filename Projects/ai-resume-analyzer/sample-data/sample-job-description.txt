Junior Data Analyst

We are looking for a Junior Data Analyst to help our product team turn data into actionable insights.

Responsibilities:
- Write SQL queries to extract and validate data.
- Analyze product usage with Python and pandas.
- Build dashboards in Power BI or Tableau.
- Present clear recommendations to cross-functional stakeholders.

Qualifications:
- Strong SQL and spreadsheet skills.
- Familiarity with Python-based data analysis.
- Experience with dashboard and visualization tools.
- Excellent written and verbal communication.
- Knowledge of A/B testing is a plus.
